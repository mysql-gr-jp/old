Hi!

Thank you for your report. We have fixed a bug and fix will come in
3.23.50. 

The problem is that in configure.in we forgot to add:

AC_SUBST(ENABLE_LOCAL_INFILE)

We have now fixed this in the MySQL 3.23 source tree..

The fix is to add

[client]
local-infile=1

to /etc/my.cnf.
